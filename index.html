3. Third-Party Services & Data Safety

To ensure secure and high-performance operations, we integrate trusted third-party services which handle data according to their own strict privacy rules:

4. System Permissions & Data Minimization

We believe in data minimization. We only declare and request the absolute minimum permissions required for basic app utility:

  • INTERNET (android.permission.INTERNET): Required to stream high-quality devotional audio tracks and load HD live wallpapers from our secure servers.
  • WAKE_LOCK (android.permission.WAKE_LOCK): Necessary to keep the audio processor active during meditation or playback when the screen dims.
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE (android.permission.FOREGROUND_SERVICE) & FOREGROUND_SERVICE_MEDIA_PLAYBACK: Essential to allow background audio playback (e.g., listening to Mantras) to continue uninterrupted when you navigate to other apps or lock your device.
